Lab 4: Image Filtering

Thu 9 February 2023 by Aditya Arora

The goal of this lab is to implement filtering methods for the game Where's Waldo. In the drive, you will find multiple high-resolution images with a hiding Waldo inside. The corresponding images of Waldo are also given (with the corresponding number, i.e., waldo2.png goes with wheres_waldo2.jpeg).

Load the images using OpenCV. Use plt.imshow(img) to visualize your images.

Using the waldoX.png image as the filter and wheres_waldoX.jpg as the image, your job is to implement the following:

Using only OpenCV and PyTorch, implement three filtering algorithms: (i) convolution, (ii) correlation, and (iii) normalized cross correlation. Use these to find Waldo in at least one of the images. Why will only one of these work to find Waldo?

As usual, let the TA know when you are done!
